ABOUT

I was born and raised in Yangon, Myanmar, received my education from B.E.H.S (2) Kamayut. I enjoy spending my free time reading, drawing, and crafting. I currently reside in Monrovia, California, where I'm pursuing my Bachelor's in Media Arts & Graphic Design.

Currently I am freelance graphic design professional and I offer a proven ability to conduct creative analysis and design best-fit solutions for multi-faceted campaigns including photo retouching, digital photo manipulation and digital illustration. I have assisted clients from all backgrounds and industries to design and implement thoughtful and impactful Branding tools to help grow their business.
